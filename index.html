ABOUT US

We are creators and producers of social and cultural events especialized in thematic events for children, enterprises, exhibitions, festivals and others.

Our business started over a decade ago in Mexico, delivering an optimal service accomplishing clients expectations.

Now we are proud about launching our project in LA.

SERVICES

As producers, we make your event shine, making sure that all elements of it are well covered and set up.

We are constantly creating and modifying our shows in order to accomplish the client necessities, so you can feel free to ask for anything that you would like to introduce in your event and we will try to make it possible.

In our staff premises we provide clowns, magicians, enterteiners, face painters, characters (botargas), circus shows, stilt walkers, and much more.
